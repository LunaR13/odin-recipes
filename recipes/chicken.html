Buttermilk Chicken Recipe

Description

Buttermilk Chicken perfectly compliments chicken by making it sweeter

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 1/2) pound chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dired rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon sage
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups Buttermilk

Steps

  1. Toss together chicken pieces, black pepper, salt, paprika, rosemary, thyme, oregano, sage, white pepper, and cayenne in a large bowl to coat.
